The Not So Mysterious Daily Update Website Journal Diary Thing

10/27/25 - 10:51 PM CDT - The Not So Mysterious Daily Update Website Journal Diary Thing I Can't Believe I Pasted This Like 5 Times, Plus The Captalization Is Completely Incorrect But I Don't Feel Like Changing It

Having another person perceive this website is horrifying, but it's real and it's happening as we speak.

Thinking about the "why" of this website. Why did I make this? Why have I subjected myself to this? I really can't answer. I guess it's because it's been the most consistent thing in my life. Every day an update. Every once in a while, I forget or don't feel like updating, but I still update. This routine and the website itself have stayed the same for all of these three years. I think I've changed, but I probably haven't. Although nowadays reading the sentence,

"NNN, I guess. I'm not a guy, but if I was, I still wouldn't care about the "challenge". I already immediately failed No Simp September, lol. N" (Update 18)

amuses me, knowing vital pieces of information that I did not on November 1st, 2022. So in that sense I suppose I have changed. But let's discuss a brief history of this website.

It was the 8th grade, a few months after I'd moved states. In the first quarter, I was in a mandatory computer class. In that class we did a course on the website code.org, in which we learned the basics of HTML and CSS. I was intensely fixated on this. I made a website I thought was extremely funny (it wasn't) about how to cook a pizza with pineapple on top. Riveting. I remember showing it to my teacher and I think she thought it was funny. Or she was confused. Probably the latter. But anyway, I had a lot of fun creating that website, so I created two other websites. A website about old slang I was fixated on and a website in which I had asked a random person on ... Discord for an idea and went with it. And then I made this website.

This is all to say that I don't really remember exactly why I made this website. Perhaps I got the idea from a website or a video and that combined with my brief coding fixation led to this. I can't be sure about any of these details.

Trying to find information I browse messages from my old friend group's Discord server (that was really my server, because I was all full of myself). I find that I sent the server a link to this website. Multiple times. Every time I updated I sent a link. And when I found this I decided to delete every single instance of this website from the server. Nobody has talked there in two years, but there's still people in there, so I felt I had to. In these messages I see that there once was a comments section, but I deleted it after a friend posted my legal name. Fantastic.

Considering all of this, I suppose that there once was an audience for this website. Maybe an audience that I pretended existed from my friend group. But still, an audience. Maybe I wanted to be a cool blogger. I recall intending to write actual blogposts, but I never got to doing so. I'm not sure why I never did. Perhaps I just forgot. It's little aspects of my life like this website that prove that time eludes me.

There's probably an update or two where I'm practically screaming through the screen about something unimportant that happened at school that annoyed me. Maybe three. Probably a lot more, actually. And now someone is perceiving this all.
